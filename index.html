Forthcoming book: A New Sun Temple at Tell el-Amarna by Jacquelyn Williamson

This monograph will be published in two volumes.
The first volume will include an analysis of the site of Kom el-Nana, including its context in the reign of the King Akhenaten and Queen Nefertiti, circa 1450 BCE. It presents reconstructions of the walls of the North Shrine area, and provides an in depth discussion of the mathmatic calculations required to achieve the reconstructions. The book also discusses the role of Nefertiti in the reign of Akhenaten as illuminated by new information from the relief and inscriptions discovered at Kom el-Nana.
The second volume, intended to serve as a reference for other scholars, will include photographs of all the diagnostic, decorated stone fragments from the North Shrine at Kom el-Nana.
